FAQs

Rider's FAQ

Routes is a carpooling mobile application that seamlessly connects passengers to drivers going to the same destination thereby providing safe affordable and timely rides for passengers.

  1. Open your app and enter your destination in the ‘Where to?’ field, you will be redirected to the nearest bus stops to your destination
  2. Confirm your pickup location by selecting the nearest bus stop to you.
  3. Select ‘Confirm Pickup’ to send your request to eligible drivers in your area.
  4. Once a driver accepts your request, you will see their location, estimated time of arrival, driver and vehicle details.
  5. ROUTES will send you notifications when your driver is close to your pickup bus stop.

Fares are calculated using time and distance of a trip. Toll fees are added to some trips. When there are more riders than available drivers, prices temporarily increase until supply and demand are rebalanced.

Sample price estimates do not reflect variations due to discounts, traffic delays or other factors. Actual prices may vary.

Routes is presently available in Lagos Nigeria. With Routes, you can request a ride in any bus stop in Lagos 24 hours a day, 7 days a week.

The ROUTES app cannot match you with a specific driver. When you request a ride, your app sends your request to nearby drivers to pick you up at your pickup bus stop.

Pickup location refers to the nearest major bus stop closest to you at the time of making a ride request.

Drop-off location refers to the nearest major bus stops closest to your destination.

If you wish to contact your driver about a pickup location or talk about other details of the ride you requested, you can do that through the app. To contact your driver:

  1. Tap the bar that displays the driver’s information.
  2. Select contact to choose between calling or messaging your driver

Note: Drivers may not answer their phones or reply to a text while driving.

You can cancel a trip at any time through the ROUTES app, but may be charged a cancellation fee if you cancel after you are matched with your driver. Cancellation fees pay drivers for the time and effort they spend in getting to your location. Ride requests should only be made when you are ready and near the pick up bus stop. This ensures smooth pickups for riders and drivers.

You won’t be charged a cancellation fee if:

  1. Your driver took much longer than expected to arrive.
  2. ROUTES detects that your driver isn’t making much progress to your pickup bus stop.

You will be charged a cancellation fee if:

  1. You cancel a few minutes after a driver accepts the trip.
  2. Your driver cancels after waiting for more than 5 minutes at your pickup bus stop.

You can select a payment method for a ride while booking for the ride. You can choose between cash or debit card payment.

Ratings allow us to ensure a great experience for both riders and drivers. We take ratings very seriously. In the app at the end of each trip, or at the bottom of your emailed receipt, you will be able to rate your driver from 1 to 5 stars. You can also provide specific feedback about the trip or driver. Certain issues (fare amount, app issues etc.) are not your driver’s fault and should not be selected as reasons for a low rating. Drivers will never see the specific rating you give.

When a trip ends, we automatically send a receipt to the email address on your ROUTES account. If you aren’t receiving receipts, check the spam or junk folder of this email address. You can review your email address by selecting ‘Settings’ from your app menu. Tap your email address to update it.

Routes rider’s app has a panic button that sends details of your ride that includes your current location, your destination, driver’s name, vehicle details to three contacts that you’ve provided as your emergency contact. The panic button also alerts us to track the driver’s phone and monitor the ride.

Driver's FAQ

Routes is a carpooling mobile application that seamlessly connects passengers to drivers going to the same destination thereby providing safe affordable and timely rides for passengers.

The application process takes anywhere from 2 to 5 working days after submission of your online application.

Drivers are paid in two ways, either through cash after a passenger pays with cash for a trip or through transfers to the bank account every week. Drivers earn 70% of the fare and are allowed a maximum of 10,000 naira cash cap after which they are to remit 30% of the fare to Routes before they are allowed to accept new rides.

At the top of the screen, slide the steering wheel icon to the right to go online. This lets you receive ride requests. To see what different requests looks like in the app, skip to How rides appear in the app. Once you're online, follow these steps:

  1. When you get a ride request, you'll see a notification with the passenger's name, pickup ETA, and ride type. Tap anywhere to accept.
  2. Tap the arrow next to the pickup location
  3. Select 'Tap to arrive' when you’re at the pickup location. Tap 'Confirm arrival' to send the rider a text (if we haven't already).
  4. Tap 'Pick up (passenger's name)' when the rider gets in to start the ride.
  5. Tap 'Navigate' to begin navigation, then drive the rider to their destination.
  6. Tap 'Tap to drop off' when you arrive at the drop-off location, then tap 'Confirm drop off' to end the ride.
  7. Tap the star icon to rate the passenger manually, or let the timer finish to auto-rate the passenger 5 stars. That's it
