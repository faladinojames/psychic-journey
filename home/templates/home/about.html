About Us

Connect Routes is a ride sharing company seeking to make day-to-day commuting hassle free. With its headquarters in Lagos, Nigeria, Routes, an on-demand carpooling platform seamlessly connects passengers to drivers going in the same destination. Routes also connects passengers to drivers going through their destination. All this is done via our mobile application intelligently designed to have a user-friendly interface.

Our mission is to constantly provide safe, affordable, timely and convenient rides for passengers for their daily commute while also empowering our drivers with a source of income and ensuring they operate with utmost professionalism.

At Routes, we believe in ease of transportation and constantly try to make this a reality for everyone on our platform. We have an amazing team of bright minds who are obsessed with finding new ways to make navigating through Nigerian roads less challenging.
